Drawing

Drawing helps improve hand-eye coordination and sharpens your attention to detail. By observing proportions, light, and shadow, you can create visually stunning artworks. Regular practice in drawing builds discipline and enhances your ability to translate ideas into tangible visual expressions.


Designing

Design is about finding creative solutions to visual or functional challenges. By using principles of composition, color theory, and user experience, you can design products, spaces, or visuals that not only look great but also serve a meaningful purpose and enhance user satisfaction.


Fashion (Clothing Design)

Fashion design requires a keen sense of detail, from fabric selection to fit and finishing touches. Understanding how to match textures, patterns, and colors, as well as constructing garments with precision, leads to stylish and well-made clothing that reflects personal and cultural trends.
